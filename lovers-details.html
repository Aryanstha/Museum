Lovers in the Waves

  1. Home
  2. Exhibition
  3. lovers-details

Art information

  • Creator: Edward Munch
  • Date Created: 1896
  • Rights: Gift of Charles and Evelyn Kramer, New York, to the American Friends of Tel Aviv Museum of Art, 1986 Â©Tel Aviv Museum of Art
  • Medium: Lithograph

It portrays a couple in a passionate embrace, standing in the shallow waters of a beach as the waves crash around them. The figures are depicted in a stylized and simplified manner, with bold brushstrokes and bright, contrasting colors. The painting is a powerful representation of the intensity and intimacy of romantic love, as well as the timeless and elemental power of nature. The piece is considered a masterpiece of Munch's mature style and a prime example of his expressionistic approach to art.
