Welcome to the Virtual Museum of Israeli Art.

An online exhibition space that showcases the rich and diverse artistic traditions of Israel. Our virtual museum is a unique platform that allows visitors from around the world to explore Israel's art and cultural heritage from the comfort of their own homes.

Museum's collection

The museum's collection features a wide range of artistic styles and media, including painting, sculpture, photography, and video art. We highlight the works of both established and emerging Israeli artists, offering a glimpse into the vibrant and dynamic contemporary art scene in Israel.

Virtual exhibitions

Our virtual exhibitions are curated to provide a comprehensive and engaging experience for visitors. Each exhibition is carefully selected and presented to tell a story about Israel's artistic traditions, cultural history, and social context. Our exhibitions explore a wide range of themes, from the intersection of art and politics, to the impact of Israeli art on global contemporary art trends.

Vast collection of artwork

Our virtual museum features a vast collection of artworks from different periods and styles, including paintings, sculptures, photographs, and installations.

Range of interactive features

You can zoom in on artworks to see details up close, read informative labels and descriptions, and listen to audio commentary from our curators and artists.

Virtual galleries

Offer a range of online events and programs, including talks, workshops, and tours.

Range of services for educators

We also offer a range of services for educators, students, and researchers, including educational resources, study guides, and access to our digital archives.
